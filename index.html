Strategy Overview

This strategy uses a combination of Moving Averages and the Relative Strength Index (RSI) to identify buy and sell signals.

Indicators

  • 10-period Moving Average (MA)
  • 50-period Moving Average (MA)
  • 14-period Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Entry Signals

  • Buy Signal: 10-period MA crosses above 50-period MA, and RSI is above 50.
  • Sell Signal: 10-period MA crosses below 50-period MA, and RSI is below 50.

Stop-Loss and Take-Profit

  • Stop-Loss: Place at the recent swing low (for buy) or swing high (for sell).
  • Take-Profit: Aim for a risk-reward ratio of at least 1:1.5.

Risk Management

Calculate your position size based on your risk tolerance. For example, with a $500 account, risk 1% ($5) per trade.

Daily Routine

  • Market Analysis: Check economic calendars for news impacting EUR/USD and GBP/USD.
  • Trade Execution: Look for entry signals during the London and New York sessions.
  • Review: Journal and review your trades daily.
